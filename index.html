Skills England AU0009 · AU0010 · AU0011 · Level 5

Three units. Fifteen topics. A clean progression.

Our Chief Academic Officer has sequenced AU0009, AU0010 and AU0011 as a single arc: Strategy & Opportunity → Adoption, Procurement & Governance → Delivery & Organisational Transformation. Each unit is 30 hours, levy-eligible, synchronous, and starts May 2026.

UNIT 01 · AU0009

AI Strategy and Opportunity

Discover AI transformation opportunities and create AI transformation strategies.

  • 01AI opportunity landscape and organisational needExplore the current AI tool landscape, link AI capability to your business model and organisational goals, and define business needs, use cases and requirements.
  • 02AI solution risk assessmentCompare opportunities to strategic AI risks and become able to identify best areas for AI adoption in your company.
  • 03AI-assisted solution prototypingAssess and demonstrate AI potential in your business through rapid prototyping and piloting.
  • 04Monitor AI changes effectivelyKeep yourself constantly up-to-date with global AI developments and news without becoming overwhelmed, focusing on what is useful for your use cases and your organisation.
  • 05Get stakeholder buy-inCommunicate AI strategies to stakeholders (both technical and non-technical staff) for organisational buy-in.

UNIT 02 · AU0010

AI Adoption, Procurement and Governance

Design specific AI transformation plans.

  • 01Translating strategy into an implementation planDesign a detailed plan, involving the selection of specific AI tools, for implementing strategies confirmed in the previous apprenticeship unit.
  • 02AI tool provider procurementDeep-dive into comparisons between main providers and solutions, understanding their strengths and weaknesses.
  • 03Engaging stakeholders in AI tool selectionInvolve relevant stakeholders into testing of potential AI solutions to ensure later cooperation.
  • 04Compliance, legal and ethical preparednessAssess specific compliance, legal, ethical risks, find ways to mitigate them, ensuring it’s reflected in the implementation plans.
  • 05Preparing for AI-driven workforce changesPlan for changes in roles of your organisation, maximising how AI can empower staff in their tasks and creating a future-state role map for your organisation.

UNIT 03 · AU0011

AI Delivery and Organisational Transformation

Lead and implement AI-enabled change strategies with confidence.

  • 01Implementing AI transformationImplement the AI transformation strategies developed in the previous apprenticeship unit.
  • 02Supporting your organisation through AI transformationPractice supporting roles undergoing changes due to AI transformation, handle potential resistance and ambiguity.
  • 03Monitoring for bias, drift, and securityMonitor and prevent risks of AI systems in bias, drift, and security and know how to respond to identified issues based on their severity.
  • 04Adapting to changing AI regulation environmentsRespond to regulatory changes and compliance incidents affecting AI systems already in production.
  • 05Identifying risk points during AI transformationIdentify early and late-stage signs of successful and at-risk AI transformation processes.

Learner criteria

  • ●
    Lives in EnglandLearner must live in England and spend at least 50% of their working time there.
  • ●
    Leadership roleMiddle manager leading a team, or C-suite / senior leader equivalent.
  • ●
    New content to the roleLess than 50% of the unit content already covered in the learner's current role or past training.
